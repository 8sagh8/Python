=== TIC TAC TOE  GAME ===

it is a simple game to play with computer
each box have a value

  1 | 2 | 3 
 --- --- ---
  4 | 5 | 6
 --- --- --- 
  7 | 8 | 9

pick a value on your turn to mark "X"

for example: if you picked value 5, it will draw board like as below:

  1 | 2 | 3 
 --- --- ---
  4 | X | 6
 --- --- --- 
  7 | 8 | 9

This game is not an example of Artifitial Intelligence, therefore, all
computer moves are randomly choiced not intended to make user loose or draw
